CHICKPEA WITH RICE

Description

This is my favorite dish to eat, only if my mother makes it

Ingredients
  • Chickpea soaked overnight
  • Steamed rice
  • lots of wierd spices
Steps
  1. Take your chickpeas, wash them and submerge them in water overnight
  2. Prepare the rice next day
  3. Create a spice mix(Mumma knows that step)
  4. Cook the Chickpea stew/gravy/curry
  5. Enjoy with rice
